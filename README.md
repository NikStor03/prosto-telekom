### Techincal Ditails

The system is built on FastAPI, Next.js and PostgreSQL as the core relational database. Artificial intelligence features are powered by OpenAI models, enabling advanced automation, decision-making, and natural language processing. This technology stack ensures high performance, reliability, scalability, and rapid product iteration.

Our system adapts to the specific needs of your business: it automates order processing, manages and screens job applicants, selects the best employees, tracks company KPIs, and monitors individual tasks and employee performance.
